FAQ

What is customer-funded growth?
Customer-funded growth means expanding the company by reinvesting revenue from paying customers rather than external venture capital. This requires an optimized sales process and a strong focus on cash flow.
How does Growth-as-a-Service work?
It is an arrangement where Calle steps in operationally in your organization for a period to drive growth projects. Unlike a traditional consultant who leaves a report, Calle stays for the execution.
Which industries does Calle have experience in?
I have deep experience in B2B, with specific expertise in SaaS, software, and sales of complex services (Professional Services). My strategy works best where deals are built on trust and long-term relationships, whether it involves digital products or consultative solutions.

Right conditions for right results – are we a good match?

To me, growth isn't about overconsumption, but about creating resources for meaningful work and a more sustainable world. I believe in the power of teamwork and diversity, and that together we can actually change things for the better.

Therefore, I work exclusively with businesses that want to move forward – not just maintain the status quo. I am careful that my collaborations align with the conviction that every deal should create value both for the bottom line and for the world in the long term.
